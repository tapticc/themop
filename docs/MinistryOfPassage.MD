# The Ministry of Passage --- Gameplay Example

## Concept

The Ministry of Passage is a fictional infrastructure organisation
providing:

Storage services\
Travel permits\
Governance roles\
Contribution tracking

The goal is to demonstrate how players could build persistent
organisations using Sui.

---

## Gameplay Loop

Deposit Items → Earn Points → Gain Rank → Purchase Permits → Access
Infrastructure

---

## Key Gameplay Systems

### Ministry Storage Units

Players deposit recovered items and receive:

Compliance Points\
Ministry Points

Demonstrates:

• Storage extensions\
• Inventory inspection\
• Event scoring

---

### Ministry Gates

Custom gate extensions require permits purchased with compliance points.

Demonstrates:

• Gate extension witnesses\
• Permit issuance\
• Permit consumption during travel

---

### Citizenship Model

Players earning points can gain:

Citizen role

Unlocking permissions such as:

Infrastructure access\
Governance visibility

---

## Why This Example Matters

Demonstrates how Eve Frontier could support:

Player governments\
Infrastructure providers\
Reputation systems\
Service organisations

---

## Design Goal

Show how Sui Move supports:

Systems \> Assets\
Governance \> Tokens\
Infrastructure \> speculation

---

## Key Takeaway

The Ministry demonstrates how Sui enables **player societies**, and is tribe-agnostic.
